Top Ranking CARE MEDICAL COLLEGE HOSPITAL

CARe Medical College is one of the finest private medical college around the country. The word ‘CARe’ of the CARe Medical College is stands for ‘Centre for Assisted Reproduction’ which was established as a tertiary centre for infertility treatment in November, 1999 at Shamoli, Dhaka. This was the first institute of its kind in the country equipped with all sorts of latest technologies required for ‘In-Vitro Fertilization’. The first IVF babies in Bangladesh ‘Heera, Moni, Mukta (triplets)’were born on 30th May, 2001 in this centre. The first ‘ICSI’(intra-cytoplasmic sperm injection’ baby ‘Persa’ was born on 20th June in the same year. This achievement was possible for firm determination and relentless effort of Prof. Dr. Parveen Fatima, a renowned Gynaecologist and Infertility specilialist and Prof. Dr. Md. Moazzam Hossain, an Embryologist as well as a Paediatrician.

Considering the difficulties and sufferings of the patients to get medical services from different places, we decided to deliver all types of health services under one roof. With this idea in mind, initially a ten-storied building with 2 basements (72,000 sqft.) was built in 2005 at 2/1 Iqbal Road, Mohammadpur named ‘CARe Hospital’, purposefully built as a hospital. This is a centrally air-conditioned building and equipped with all sorts of modern technical facilities required for health services. The state-of-the-art IVF Laboratory, Operation Theatres, NICU, PICU and other facilities related to Mother and Child Health care are the prime attractions of this hospital. The location of the hospital is easily approachable, fascinating ,on the main road and adjacent to Sangsad Bhaban and GonoBhaban. In 2010, another ten-storied hospital building with two basements (70,000sqft.) having 250 beds was started on the main road at College Gate just opposite Sohrawardi Medical College & Hospital. It was named as ‘CARe Specialized Hospital and Research Centre’. This building is also centrally air-conditioned having emergency, outpatient department, general wards, cabins, ICU, HDU, Haemodialysis unit, Modular operation theatres, investigation facilities like CTScan, MRI, digital X-rays, 4-D ultrasonogram and so on.
